Learnin' the Ropes

Welcome to Learnin' the Ropes!
In this game you will be given many multiple choice questions in popup boxes. When you are done reading a question/its answer choices and are ready to play, click OK on the popup.

The background and your character will then start to move. You will have to quickly click on the rope that has the letter corresponding to the correct answer. The letters appear in a random order so be quick!

Watch out for asteroids!

Each correct answer is worth 2 points, each wrong answer is -1 point, and getting hit by the asteroid is -1 point.

Happy learnin'!
